中文隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 15 日

我们不收集任何个人数据。取色器的取色、图片分析与配色计算均在您的 Mac 本地完成。

1. 我们处理的信息

取色器不要求注册或登录，也不会收集姓名、邮箱、设备标识、位置、使用记录或其他个人信息。

2. 屏幕取色

屏幕取色使用 macOS 提供的系统取色器 NSColorSampler。App 只接收您最终选择的颜色值，不录制、保存或上传屏幕画面。

3. 图片取色

只有在您主动使用系统文件选择器选择图片后，App 才会以只读方式访问该图片。图片像素仅在本机内存中处理，原始图片不会被复制到服务器、上传或分享给第三方。

4. 本地存储与剪贴板

  • 取色历史与收藏保存在 App 的 macOS 沙盒容器内。
  • 上传的原始图片不会作为历史记录保存。
  • 只有在您点击复制或完成取色时，App 才会把相应颜色文本写入系统剪贴板。

5. 网络、第三方服务与跟踪

App 不连接开发者服务器，不包含广告、分析、跟踪、云端 AI 或第三方 SDK，也不会出售或共享任何用户数据。

6. 数据保留与删除

本地颜色历史会保留在 App 沙盒中，直到相关 App 数据被删除。卸载 App 后，您可以删除其容器数据以移除所有本地记录。

7. 儿童隐私

由于 App 不收集个人数据，因此不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

8. 政策更新

如果 App 的数据处理方式发生变化，我们会更新本页面及最后更新日期，并在需要时同步更新 App Store 隐私声明。

9. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系开发者。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

We do not collect personal data. Color sampling, image analysis, and palette generation are processed locally on your Mac.

1. Information We Process

Color Picker does not require an account or sign-in. It does not collect names, email addresses, device identifiers, locations, usage records, or other personal information.

2. Screen Color Sampling

Screen color sampling uses the macOS system color sampler, NSColorSampler. The App receives only the color you select and does not record, retain, or upload screen content.

3. Image Color Sampling

The App accesses an image only after you select it through the system file picker. Access is read-only. Image pixels are processed locally in memory, and the original image is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with a third party.

4. Local Storage and Clipboard

  • Color history and favorites are stored inside the App's macOS sandbox container.
  • Original imported images are not retained as history.
  • The App writes color text to the system clipboard only when you copy a value or complete a color-picking action.

5. Network Access, Third Parties, and Tracking

The App does not connect to developer-operated servers. It contains no advertising, analytics, tracking, cloud AI, or third-party SDKs, and it does not sell or share user data.

6. Retention and Deletion

Local color history remains in the App sandbox until the related App data is removed. After uninstalling the App, you may remove its container data to delete all locally stored records.

7. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the App's data practices change, we will update this page and its revision date and, where required, update the App Store privacy disclosure.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through GitHub Issues.
